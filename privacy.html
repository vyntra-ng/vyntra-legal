Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Vyntra ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Vyntra, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Vyntra is an independent skill-based gaming competition platform operated from Nigeria. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with any game developer or publisher.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: vyntrahelp@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and identity verification)
  • Username and profile information
  • Account PIN (stored in encrypted form, never readable)

Gaming Information

  • In-game name and game UID (submitted for account verification)
  • Game level and level category
  • Kill counts and match participation records

Financial Information

  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Withdrawal requests and payment records
  • Entry fee payments and match payouts

Usage Information

  • Match history and participation records
  • Device information for security purposes
  • App activity logs for fraud prevention

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your gaming identity before allowing match participation
  • Process match entry fees, payouts, and withdrawals
  • Detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and multiple accounts
  • Resolve disputes between participants
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Vyntra:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication storage
  • Paystack — payment processing for withdrawals and transactions
  • Africa's Talking — SMS delivery for OTP verification

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing their data handling. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Transaction records may be retained longer where required by applicable financial regulations.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Your PIN is hashed and never stored in readable form. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at vyntrahelp@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

Vyntra is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be suspended and their data deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Vyntra after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
